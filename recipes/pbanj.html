PB and J

Description

The classic sandwich for everyone.

Allergen warning: contains peanuts.

Ingredients

  • Bread sliced
  • Peanut butter chunky
  • Grape jelly

Steps

  1. Grab 2 slices of bread
  2. Spread peanut butter on one side
  3. Spread jelly on other side
  4. Combine bread slices
  5. Enjoy
