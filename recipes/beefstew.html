Beef Stew

Description

Hearty beef stew made with vegtables and premium beef chuck. This recipe is a family favorite that we make weekly. Pair it with noodles, rice, or anything!

For best results use a slow cooker!!!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon beef fat
  • 1 lb of premium beef chuck cubed
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1 cup Chinese cooking wine
  • 8 cloves of garlic
  • 4 cups of water
  • 2 large potatoes cubed
  • 1 large onion diced

Steps

  1. Place beef cubes at the bottom of pot
  2. Add potatoes
  3. Add onion
  4. Add garlic
  5. Add soy sauce
  6. Add cooking wine
  7. Add water
  8. Set cooker to low heat
  9. Cook for 6-8 hours stirring every 2 hours till beef breaks apart
